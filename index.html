Amazon part-time pay calculator

Enter your base hourly rate, choose the days you work, and mark each shift as morning or night. Choose each worked day's hours from 1 to 10. Morning/night only controls the night premium, and an extended shift adds 2 more hours.

Pay setup

Overtime is calculated for the fixed pay week from Sunday through Saturday.

Regular hours are paid at base rate. Night shift hours are paid 16% more, including selected hours and extended hours on night shifts. Weekly hours above 40 and up to 50 are paid at 1.5×. Hours above 50 are paid at 2×. Rolling week validation is on by default, so Saturday night followed by Sunday morning is also checked.
