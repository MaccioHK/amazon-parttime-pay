Amazon part-time pay calculator

Enter your base hourly rate, choose the days you work, and mark each shift as morning or night. Every shift counts as 8 hours, and an extended shift adds 2 more hours.

Pay setup

Overtime is calculated for the fixed pay week from Sunday through Saturday.

Regular hours are paid at base rate. Night shift hours are paid 16% more, including extended hours on night shifts. Weekly hours above 40 and up to 50 are paid at 1.5×. Hours above 50 are paid at 2×. Turn on rolling week validation to also check Saturday night followed by Sunday morning.
